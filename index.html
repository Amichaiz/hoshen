Empowering Israeli Women on the border Through Safety & Strength

In response to the events of October 7th, we're dedicated to providing Israeli women that live in communities along the border with comprehensive firearms training, safety education, and the confidence to protect themselves and their communities.

Support Our Mission Learn More

Our Mission

We believe every woman has the right to feel safe and protected.

Following the tragic events of October 7th, 2023, we recognized an urgent need to empower Israeli women especially those that live near the borders with the knowledge, skills, and confidence necessary for personal protection. Our organization provides comprehensive firearms training, safety education, and ongoing support to help women take control of their personal security.

We are committed to creating a community of strong, prepared, and confident women who can protect themselves, their families, and their communities. Through professional instruction, mental preparedness training, and ongoing support, we're building a network of empowered women across Israel.

Our Programs

Basic Firearms Training

Comprehensive introduction to firearms safety, handling, and marksmanship. Perfect for beginners with no prior experience.

Advanced Defense Tactics

Advanced training in defensive shooting, situational awareness, and tactical decision-making for experienced participants.

Community Support Groups

Peer support networks, trauma-informed counseling, and ongoing mentorship to build lasting confidence and resilience.

Legal & Ethical Training

Understanding Israeli firearms laws, self-defense regulations, and the ethical responsibilities of armed citizens.
